About

Fabric Care Guide provides practical, evidence-based guidance on fabric care and stain removal.

Scope

We focus on common household fabrics and everyday stains. Guidance varies by fabric type, dye stability, stain chemistry, and how long the stain has set.

Neutral guidance

This site is educational. It does not recommend specific brands or products. When a method depends on a product type (for example, oxygen bleach), we describe the category rather than a brand.

Safety

  • Always test any method on an inconspicuous area first.
  • Follow garment care labels and manufacturer instructions.
  • Do not mix cleaning chemicals.
  • If in doubt (delicates, antiques, or valuable items), consult a professional cleaner.
